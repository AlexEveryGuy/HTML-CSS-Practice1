Vincent Valentine Limit Breaks

Final Fantasy Wiki

Vincent Valentine as he appears in Final Fantasy VII: Advent Children

About

Vincent has only one Limit Break for each of the four levels, and when he initiates one, he turns into an uncontrollable monster and stays that way until the battle is over, or he falls into Death status, which results in him reverting. When he shifts his form, Vincent's stats increase and his HP refills, but he cannot shift back at will.

While his Limit Breaks can be powerful, they are risky, as some of the elemental attacks can heal enemies. During his transformation Vincent is immune to Berserk, Frog, and Confusion. Vincent learns his new Limit Breaks the fastest. The offensive properties of Elemental or Added Effect do not carry over to his Limit Break forms, perhaps because he is not using his weapon to attack. Defensive Materia set-ups continue to work. Vincent's Limit Break forms behave oddly under All Lucky 7s since he seems to revert to using his gun though there is no animation for it.

Vincent Limits

Galian Beast

Vincent transforms into "Galian Beast", a wild purple beast. Defense increases by 20%, and Dexterity by 50%. Sets 130% Max HP.

Ability List

  • Berserk Dance - Physical attack for 1.5 times normal damage to a single target.
  • Beast Flare - Magical attack for 3.5 times normal fire-elemental damage to all targets.

Death Gigas

Vincent transforms into "Death Gigas", a hulking monster. Defense increases by 50%, MDefense decreases by 70%, and Dexterity decreases by 20%. Sets 200% Max HP.

Ability List

  • Gigadunk - Physical attack for 2.625 times normal damage to a single target.
  • Livewire - Magical attack for 4.375 times normal lightning-elemental damage to all enemies.

Hellmasker

Vincent transforms into "Hellmask", a thug with a rotating head wearing a hockey mask who wields a chainsaw. Defense and MDefense increase by 50%.

Ability List

  • Splattercombo - Physical attack for half damage to five random targets.
  • Nightmare - Inflicts Sleep, Poison, Silence, Confusion, Frog and Small to a single opponent.

Chaos

Vincent transforms into "Chaos", a winged demon. Defense and MDefense increase by 100%.

Ability List

  • Chaos Saber - Physical attack for 2.5 times normal damage to all enemies.
  • Satan Slam - Has a 28% chance to inflict Instant Death, or if enemies are immune to death, does 5.625 times normal damage to all enemies.
